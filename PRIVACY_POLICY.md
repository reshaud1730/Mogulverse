Effective Date: March 5, 2026

Discord User ID: This is a unique numerical string used to identify your game profile and save your progress.

Your data is used strictly for the following:

Saving and loading your game progress.

Calculating daily market events and weekly leaderboard rankings.

Managing the Trickle Fund distributions for the weekly winner.

Storage: Data is stored in a secure SQLite database hosted on our private server/hosting provider.

Security: We do not share your Discord ID or game stats with third parties. Only authorized administrators of Mogulverse have access to the backend database for maintenance and reset purposes.
